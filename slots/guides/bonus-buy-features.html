1. What is Bonus Buy in Slots?

Bonus Buy (also known as Feature Buy or Buy Bonus) is a premium feature in online slots that allows players to immediately trigger a game's bonus round by paying a set multiplier of their current bet, rather than waiting for the bonus symbols to land naturally.

Key Characteristics:

  • Instant Access: Skip the base game and go straight to the bonus features
  • Premium Cost: Typically costs between 50x to 200x your current bet
  • High Volatility: Can lead to big wins but also significant losses
  • Availability: Not available in all jurisdictions due to gambling regulations

2. How Does the Bonus Buy Feature Work?

When you use the Bonus Buy feature, you're essentially paying extra to guarantee that the next spin will trigger the game's bonus round. Here's how it typically works:

1

Select Your Bet

Choose your base bet amount as you normally would

2

Click Bonus Buy

Press the Bonus Buy button (usually marked with a star or lightning bolt)

3

Enter Bonus Round

The game immediately triggers the bonus feature

Important Note

The cost of the Bonus Buy feature is in addition to your regular bet. For example, if your bet is $1 and the Bonus Buy costs 100x, you'll pay $100 to trigger the bonus.

4. Bonus Buy Strategies

While bonus buys are high-risk, high-reward features, these strategies can help you make the most of them:

1. Bankroll Management

Only use the Bonus Buy feature if you can afford to lose the cost. A good rule is to have at least 20-30 times the Bonus Buy amount in your bankroll.

Example:

If the Bonus Buy costs $100, ensure you have $2,000-$3,000 in your bankroll.

2. Compare RTP

Look for games where the Bonus Buy RTP is higher than the base game RTP. Some games increase the RTP when using the Bonus Buy feature.

4. Avoid Chasing Losses

It's easy to fall into the trap of buying multiple bonuses in a row after a loss. Set a limit on how many Bonus Buys you'll do in a session.

Warning:

The house edge is typically higher on Bonus Buys than regular spins. Use them strategically, not as a way to recover losses.

6. Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Bonus Buy feature worth it?

It depends on your playing style and bankroll. Bonus Buys offer instant access to the most exciting parts of a slot but come at a premium cost. They're best used strategically rather than as your primary way of playing.

Can I win real money with Bonus Buy?

Yes, you can win real money when using the Bonus Buy feature. The potential payouts are the same as if you had triggered the bonus through regular play, but you're paying a premium for immediate access.

Why is Bonus Buy not available in my country?

Some gambling jurisdictions, including the UK, Sweden, and Germany, have restricted or banned the Bonus Buy feature due to responsible gambling concerns. The availability depends on local gambling regulations.

What's the difference between Bonus Buy and Free Spins?

Free Spins are a type of bonus round where you get a set number of spins without placing additional bets. Bonus Buy is a feature that lets you pay to immediately trigger any type of bonus round, including free spins, pick'em games, or other special features.

Can I use Bonus Buy with bonus money?

This depends on the casino's terms and conditions. Many casinos exclude Bonus Buy features when playing with bonus money due to the high volatility and potential for quick balance depletion. Always check the terms before claiming a bonus if you plan to use the Bonus Buy feature.
