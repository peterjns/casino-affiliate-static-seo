1. What is Bonus Buy in Slots?

Bonus buy is a feature that allows you to instantly trigger a slot's bonus round by paying a premium, typically 50-100x your base bet. Instead of waiting for bonus symbols to land naturally, you can skip straight to the action.

Key Characteristics:

  • Instant access to bonus rounds
  • Cost typically ranges from 50x to 150x your bet
  • Guarantees entry to free spins or bonus games
  • Available in many modern video slots
  • Not available in all jurisdictions (banned in UK)

2. How Does the Bonus Buy Feature Work?

1

Select Your Base Bet

Choose your regular bet amount first. The bonus buy cost will be calculated as a multiple of this bet (e.g., 100x your $1 bet = $100 bonus buy).

2

Click Bonus Buy Button

Look for the bonus buy button (usually marked with a lightning bolt or "BUY" text). Click it to see the cost and confirm your purchase.

3

Enter Bonus Round

The game immediately triggers the bonus feature - usually free spins with special modifiers, multipliers, or enhanced symbols.

Important Note: Bonus buy doesn't guarantee a win. You're paying for access to the feature, not a guaranteed payout. You could still lose money even after buying the bonus.

4. Bonus Buy Strategies

1. Bankroll Management

Never spend more than 10-20% of your total bankroll on bonus buys. They're high-risk, high-reward features.

Example: With a $500 bankroll, limit bonus buys to $50-100 maximum.

2. Compare RTP

Some slots have higher RTP when using bonus buy. Check the paytable to see if the bonus buy version offers better theoretical returns.

3. Choose the Right Bonus Type

Some slots offer multiple bonus buy options. Choose based on your risk tolerance and potential payout preferences.

4. Avoid Chasing Losses

If a bonus buy doesn't pay well, resist the urge to immediately buy another. Stick to your predetermined limits.

5. Pros and Cons of Using Bonus Buy

Advantages

  • Instant Action: Skip the wait and get straight to bonus rounds
  • Time Efficient: More bonus rounds in less time
  • Higher RTP: Some slots offer better RTP with bonus buy
  • Guaranteed Feature: 100% chance to trigger the bonus

Disadvantages

  • High Cost: Expensive upfront investment
  • No Win Guarantee: Can still result in losses
  • Addictive Potential: Easy to overspend chasing wins
  • Limited Availability: Banned in some countries

6. Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Bonus Buy feature worth it?

It depends on your playing style and bankroll. If you enjoy bonus rounds and have the budget, it can provide more entertainment value. However, it doesn't improve your odds of winning.

Can I win real money with Bonus Buy?

Yes, bonus buy features can result in real money wins, but they can also result in losses. The feature guarantees access to the bonus round, not a profitable outcome.

Why is Bonus Buy not available in my country?

Some jurisdictions, including the UK, have banned bonus buy features as they're considered too similar to gambling mechanics that could increase addiction risk.

What's the difference between Bonus Buy and Free Spins?

Free spins are triggered naturally by landing scatter symbols, while bonus buy allows you to purchase immediate access to the bonus round for a premium cost.

Responsible Gaming Note

Bonus buy features can be particularly addictive due to their instant gratification nature. Always set strict limits and never chase losses. Remember that gambling should be entertainment, not a way to make money.
