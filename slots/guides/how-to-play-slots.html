1. What Are Online Slots?

Online slots are digital versions of traditional slot machines that you can play on your computer or mobile device. They feature spinning reels with various symbols, and you win by matching symbols across paylines.

Classic Slots

3-reel games with traditional symbols like fruits, bars, and sevens

Video Slots

5+ reels with advanced graphics, animations, and bonus features

Progressive Slots

Jackpot games where the prize pool increases with each bet

2. How Do Online Slots Work?

Modern online slots use a Random Number Generator (RNG) to ensure every spin is completely random and fair. Here are the key components:

Reels & Paylines

Reels are the vertical columns that spin, typically 3-5 in number. Paylines are the patterns across the reels where matching symbols must land to create a win.

RTP (Return to Player)

The percentage of all wagered money that a slot returns to players over time. Look for slots with 96%+ RTP for better long-term value.

Volatility

Measures how often and how much a slot pays. Low volatility = frequent small wins. High volatility = rare but bigger wins.
