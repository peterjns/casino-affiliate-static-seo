Our Review

Sweet Bonanza is a vibrant and colorful slot from Pragmatic Play that takes players into a candy-filled wonderland. This 6x5 grid slot uses a cluster pays system where wins are formed by landing 8 or more matching symbols anywhere on the reels, creating a unique and engaging gameplay experience.

Key Features

  • Tumble Feature: Winning symbols disappear and new ones fall down, creating potential chain reactions
  • Multiplier Bombs: Special bomb symbols can appear with multipliers from 2x to 100x
  • Free Spins: Triggered by 4 or more lollipop scatter symbols, awarding 10 free spins
  • Ante Bet: Increase your bet by 25% to double the chance of triggering free spins
  • Buy Feature: Purchase the bonus round for 100x your stake

Gameplay Experience

The game's candy theme is beautifully executed with colorful fruits and sweets adorning the reels. The tumble feature keeps the action flowing, while the multiplier bombs can appear during both base game and free spins. The free spins round is where the real magic happens, as multiplier bombs are added to the reels and don't reset between spins, leading to potentially massive wins.

Special Features

Free Spins Bonus

The free spins feature is the highlight of Sweet Bonanza. During the bonus round, multiplier bombs that land on the reels remain sticky and don't disappear after tumbles. This means multipliers can accumulate throughout the feature, leading to the game's maximum win potential of 21,100x your stake.

  • 10 free spins awarded for 4+ scatter symbols
  • Additional scatters during free spins award +5 extra spins
  • Multiplier bombs remain on reels throughout the feature
  • All multipliers are added together for final win calculation

Pros & Cons

❌ Cons

Strategy Tips

  • Ante Bet: Consider using the ante bet feature to increase free spins frequency
  • Buy Feature: Only use the buy feature if you have a large bankroll and understand the risks

  • Set Limits: Always set win and loss limits before playing
