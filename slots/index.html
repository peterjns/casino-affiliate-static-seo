Frequently Asked Questions

What is RTP in slots?

RTP (Return to Player) is a percentage that indicates how much of all wagered money a slot will pay back to players over time. For example, a slot with 96% RTP will return $96 for every $100 wagered on average.

What is volatility in slots?

Volatility measures how often and how much a slot pays out. Low volatility slots pay out smaller amounts more frequently, while high volatility slots pay out larger amounts less often.

Are online slots rigged?

No, licensed online casinos use Random Number Generators (RNGs) to ensure fair and random outcomes. Always play at licensed and regulated casinos to ensure fair play.
