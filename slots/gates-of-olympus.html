Our Review

Gates of Olympus is one of Pragmatic Play's most popular slots, featuring the mighty Zeus and the power of ancient Greek mythology. This 6x5 grid slot uses a cluster pays mechanism, where wins are formed by landing 8 or more matching symbols anywhere on the reels.

Key Features

  • Tumble Feature: Winning symbols disappear, allowing new ones to fall and create consecutive wins
  • Multiplier Symbols: Zeus symbols can appear with random multipliers from 2x to 500x
  • Free Spins: Triggered by 4 or more scatter symbols, awarding 15 free spins
  • Ante Bet: Double your bet to increase scatter symbol frequency
  • Buy Feature: Purchase the bonus round for 100x your stake

Gameplay Experience

The game's high volatility means you'll experience periods of smaller wins followed by potentially massive payouts. The multiplier feature is the real star, as multiple multipliers can combine during the tumble sequence, leading to extraordinary wins. The maximum payout of 5,000x your stake makes every spin exciting.
