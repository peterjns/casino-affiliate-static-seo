Basic Strategy Rules

Hard Hands

  • 17-21: Always stand
  • 13-16: Stand vs 2-6, hit vs 7-A
  • 12: Stand vs 4-6, hit otherwise
  • 11: Always double down
  • 10: Double vs 2-9, hit vs 10-A
  • 9: Double vs 3-6, hit otherwise
  • 8 or less: Always hit

Soft Hands

  • A,9: Always stand

Card Counting Basics

Card counting gives you a mathematical edge by tracking the ratio of high to low cards remaining in the deck.

Hi-Lo Counting System

Low Cards (2-6): +1
Neutral (7-9): 0
High Cards (10-A): -1

Keep a running count. Positive counts favor the player, negative counts favor the house.
