Understanding Casino Bonus Calculators

Casino bonus calculators are essential tools for any serious gambler. They help you determine the true value of casino bonuses by calculating wagering requirements, expected losses, and net bonus value. This guide covers everything you need to know about using these powerful tools.

What Are Wagering Requirements?

Wagering requirements (also called playthrough requirements) specify how many times you must bet your bonus amount before you can withdraw winnings. For example, a $100 bonus with 35x wagering requires $3,500 in total bets.

Key Calculator Components

  • Bonus Amount: The cash value of your casino bonus
  • Wagering Multiplier: How many times you must bet the bonus
  • House Edge: The casino's mathematical advantage per game
  • Game Contribution: How much each game type counts toward requirements
