Advanced Biometric Monitoring

Cutting-edge biometric technology in 2025 enables real-time monitoring of physiological and behavioral indicators that can signal problem gambling behaviors, providing an additional layer of protection through objective health data analysis.

❤️ Physiological Monitoring

  • Heart Rate Variability: Continuous monitoring detects stress, excitement, and anxiety levels during gaming sessions
  • Skin Temperature: Changes in body temperature can indicate emotional stress and compulsive behaviors
  • Galvanic Skin Response: Measures emotional arousal and stress through skin conductivity changes
  • Breathing Patterns: Irregular breathing can signal anxiety or compulsive gaming states

👁️ Behavioral Analysis

  • Eye Tracking: Analysis of focus patterns, blink rates, and pupil dilation to detect concentration and stress levels
  • Gesture Analysis: Detection of compulsive clicking, rapid betting, or repetitive behaviors through motion sensors
  • Click Patterns: Analysis of mouse/touch patterns to identify erratic or compulsive behaviors
  • Typing Behavior: Changes in typing speed and patterns can indicate emotional state changes
