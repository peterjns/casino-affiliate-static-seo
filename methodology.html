How We Review Online Casinos

Our expert team follows a rigorous 50-point evaluation system developed over 15+ years of industry experience. Every casino undergoes comprehensive testing before receiving our recommendation.

Our Review Process

Duration: 2-4 weeks per casino
Team: 6 expert reviewers
Criteria: 50+ evaluation points
Testing: Real money deposits

Our Testing Categories

Security & Licensing (25 points)

  • License Verification: We verify all gaming licenses with regulatory authorities
  • SSL Encryption: Testing 256-bit SSL encryption and security protocols
  • Data Protection: GDPR compliance and privacy policy analysis
  • Fair Gaming: RNG certification from eCOGRA, iTech Labs, or GLI
  • Financial Security: Segregated player funds and audit reports

Games & Software (20 points)

  • Game Variety: Slots, table games, live dealer options
  • Software Providers: Quality and reputation of game developers
  • RTP Rates: Return to Player percentages verification
  • Mobile Compatibility: iOS and Android testing
  • Game Performance: Loading times and stability testing

Bonuses & Promotions (15 points)

  • Welcome Bonus Value: Bonus amount and wagering requirements
  • Terms Analysis: Hidden clauses and fair play conditions
  • Ongoing Promotions: Loyalty programs and regular offers
  • Bonus Abuse Prevention: Fair terms without predatory practices
  • Cashback Programs: VIP rewards and comp point systems

Payment Methods (15 points)

  • Deposit Options: Credit cards, e-wallets, crypto, bank transfers
  • Withdrawal Speed: Processing times for different methods
  • Transaction Fees: Hidden costs and currency conversion
  • Withdrawal Limits: Daily, weekly, and monthly limits
  • KYC Process: Account verification requirements

Customer Support (10 points)

  • Availability: 24/7 support and response times
  • Contact Methods: Live chat, email, phone support
  • Language Support: Multi-language customer service
  • Knowledge Base: FAQ and self-help resources
  • Problem Resolution: Complaint handling effectiveness

User Experience (5 points)

  • Website Design: Navigation and visual appeal
  • Registration Process: Account creation simplicity
  • Search Functionality: Game filtering and search
  • Responsible Gambling: Self-exclusion and limit tools
  • Terms Clarity: Clear and accessible terms

Our Rating System

5.0: Exceptional (90-100 points)
4.5: Excellent (80-89 points)
4.0: Very Good (70-79 points)
3.5: Good (60-69 points)

Note: We only recommend casinos scoring 3.5 stars or higher. Casinos below this threshold are flagged with warnings or blacklisted.

Editorial Standards & Ethics

  • Independence: No casino can pay for better ratings or reviews
  • Transparency: All affiliate relationships are clearly disclosed
  • Regular Updates: Reviews updated quarterly or when significant changes occur
  • Real Testing: All reviewers make real deposits and test withdrawal processes
  • Fact Checking: All claims verified with casino operators and regulatory bodies
  • User Feedback: Reader comments and experiences incorporated into reviews
