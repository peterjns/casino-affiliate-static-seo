How to Play Blackjack: Complete 2025 Strategy Guide

Learn how to play blackjack with perfect basic strategy and reduce the house edge to just 0.28% - 0.5%

Game Basics

  • Objective: Get as close to 21 as possible without going over
  • Card Values: Aces = 1 or 11, Face cards = 10, Numbers = face value
  • House Edge: 0.5% - 2% depending on rules and strategy
  • RTP: 98% - 99.5% with perfect basic strategy

Pro-Level Basic Strategy (99.5% RTP)

  • Hard 17+: Always stand
  • Hard 11: Always double down (except vs Ace in some variants)
  • Pairs of Aces/8s: Always split
  • Pairs of 10s/5s: Never split
  • Soft 18 vs dealer 9-A: Hit (most players get this wrong)
  • 16 vs 10: Hit (surrender if available)
  • 15 vs 10: Hit (surrender if available)
  • A,7 vs 2: Double if allowed, otherwise stand

Baccarat Strategy Guide

Master the elegant game of baccarat with proven strategies and expert tips

Game Basics

Objective

Bet on which hand (Player or Banker) will have a total closest to 9, or if they'll tie.

Card Values

  • Ace: 1 point
  • 2-9: Face value
  • 10, J, Q, K: 0 points
  • Total over 9: Drop the first digit (e.g., 15 = 5)

Third Card Rules

  • Player: Draws on 0-5, stands on 6-7
  • Banker: Complex rules based on Player's third card
  • Natural: 8 or 9 total stops all drawing

Betting Options & Payouts

Banker Bet

Payout: 1:1 (minus 5% commission)

House Edge: 1.06%

Best Bet: Lowest house edge

Player Bet

Payout: 1:1 (no commission)

House Edge: 1.24%

Second Best: Slightly higher edge

Tie Bet

Payout: 8:1 or 9:1

House Edge: 14.4%

Avoid: Very high house edge

Advanced Baccarat Strategies

Edge Sorting Technique

  • Method: Identify card back pattern variations
  • Target Cards: Focus on 6, 7, 8, 9 identification
  • Advantage: 6.76% edge when executed properly
  • Requirement: Automatic shuffler with consistent orientation

Banker Streak Exploitation

  • Pattern: Banker wins 50.68% vs Player 49.32%
  • Strategy: Bet Banker after 3+ consecutive Player wins
  • Progression: 1-2-4 unit betting on Banker streaks
  • Stop Loss: After 3 consecutive losses

Card Counting System

  • Count: +1 for A,2,3 | -1 for 10,J,Q,K | 0 for 4-9
  • Bet Banker: When true count is +16 or higher
  • Bet Player: When true count is -16 or lower
  • Edge: 0.6% advantage at extreme counts

Optimal Bankroll Management
