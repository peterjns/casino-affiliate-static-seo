Blackjack Strategy Guide

Master basic strategy and reduce the house edge to as low as 0.5%

Game Basics

  • Objective: Get as close to 21 as possible without going over
  • Card Values: Aces = 1 or 11, Face cards = 10, Numbers = face value
  • House Edge: 0.5% - 2% depending on rules and strategy
  • RTP: 98% - 99.5% with perfect basic strategy

Basic Strategy Rules

  • Hard 17+: Always stand
  • Hard 11: Always double down
  • Pairs of Aces/8s: Always split
  • Pairs of 10s/5s: Never split
  • Soft 18 vs dealer 9-A: Hit

Advanced Tips

Card Counting Basics

Hi-Lo system: +1 for 2-6, 0 for 7-9, -1 for 10-A. Higher counts favor the player.

Bankroll Management

Never bet more than 5% of your bankroll on a single hand. Set win/loss limits.

Common Mistakes

Avoid insurance bets, don't chase losses, and never deviate from basic strategy based on "hunches".

Texas Hold'em Poker Guide

Learn the fundamentals of the world's most popular poker variant

Game Structure

Betting Rounds

  1. Pre-flop: Two hole cards dealt
  2. Flop: Three community cards revealed
  3. Turn: Fourth community card revealed
  4. River: Fifth community card revealed

Hand Rankings (High to Low)

  • 1. Royal Flush (A-K-Q-J-10 suited)
  • 2. Straight Flush
  • 3. Four of a Kind
  • 4. Full House
  • 5. Flush
  • 6. Straight
  • 7. Three of a Kind
  • 8. Two Pair
  • 9. One Pair
  • 10. High Card

Basic Strategy

Starting Hands

Premium: AA, KK, QQ, AK suited

Strong: JJ, 10-10, AQ, AK offsuit

Playable: 99-77, suited connectors, A-x suited

Position Strategy

Early Position: Play tight, premium hands only

Middle Position: Expand range slightly

Late Position: Play wider range, steal blinds

Betting Strategy

Bet for value with strong hands, bluff selectively, and always consider pot odds when calling.

Roulette Strategy Guide

Master European and American roulette with optimal betting strategies

Game Variants

European Roulette

  • Numbers: 1-36 + single 0
  • House Edge: 2.7%
  • RTP: 97.3%
  • Best Choice: Lower house edge

American Roulette

  • Numbers: 1-36 + 0 + 00
  • House Edge: 5.26%
  • RTP: 94.74%
  • Avoid: Higher house edge

Bet Types & Payouts

  • Straight Up: 35:1 (single number)
  • Split: 17:1 (two numbers)
  • Street: 11:1 (three numbers)
  • Red/Black: 1:1 (even money)
  • Odd/Even: 1:1 (even money)

Betting Systems

Martingale System

Strategy: Double bet after each loss

Risk: Requires large bankroll, table limits

D'Alembert System

Strategy: Increase bet by 1 unit after loss, decrease by 1 after win

Risk: Lower risk than Martingale

Fibonacci System

Strategy: Follow Fibonacci sequence for bet sizing

Risk: Moderate progression system

Important Note

No betting system can overcome the house edge. All systems are for entertainment and bankroll management only.
